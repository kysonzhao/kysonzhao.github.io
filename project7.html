"This is a video I've created that depicts the transformation of the night sky over the course of twelve months. The video begins in January, showcasing a clear winter night, dotted with constellations and the bright gleam of distant planets. As the video progresses, viewers can observe the gradual shift of celestial bodies, reflecting the Earth's movement around the sun.Each month presents a unique tableau of the night sky. From the blossoming of spring constellations to the rich, starry skies of summer, the celestial ballet continues into the golden hues of autumn and finally returns to the crisp clarity of winter.
