New Species

Pencil

Photoshop

In my artwork titled 'New Species', I've creatively merged the characteristics of a lizard and a butterfly, bestowing the lizard with the fantastical ability to fly. This artistic fusion not only blurs the line between the terrestrial and the aerial, but also challenges the conventional understanding of these creatures, resulting in a visually stunning and conceptually intriguing new entity.
