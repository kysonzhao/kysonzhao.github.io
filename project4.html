AlphabetDesign

illustrator

Photoshop

"My Alphabet Design project is an intriguing exploration of the English alphabet, where each of the 24 letters is uniquely represented through captivating blue motifs. By using different shades and patterns of blue, I've imbued every letter with its own personality, creating a visually cohesive yet individually distinctive suite of designs. This project marries language and visual art, transforming the familiar letters of the alphabet into a spectrum of artistic expressions that evoke feelings of tranquility and depth, much like the color blue itself."
